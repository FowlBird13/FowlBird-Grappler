Grappler Description:
Grappler is a brawling melee character with high mobility abilities. Cooldown
management is essential to not leave yourself stranded in a tricky situation.

Passive:
    Option One (Adrenaline Rush):
        Killing an enemy reduces cooldowns by 0.5s, but all of your base 
        cooldowns are very long.
    Option Two (Fervor):
        The secondary ability reduces the cooldown of the Mobility Ability
        by 50% if it hits an enemy. Increases by 5% for each additional enemy 
        hit after the first. Increase the cooldown of just the Mobility Ability.
    Option Three (Ignore Pain):
        Blocking damage with the Special Ability, reduces the cooldown of the mobility
        ability. Increase the cooldown of just the mobility ability.
    Option Four (...):
        Nothing. All of abilities have normal cooldowns.

Primary (Rule of Three):
    A three-hit combo ending with a short dash forward. The damage dealt will 
    be weak and the combo will be slow. The intention is to provide a 
    last-resort piece of mobility while all of the other abilities are on 
    cooldown.

Secondary (Brutal Belay):
    Whip the hook forward, piercing enemies. Enemies hit are pulled until they
    are directly in front of you. Does NOT stun.

Mobility (Acrobatic Mantle):
    Throw the hook forward tethering and stunning the first enemy hit. Pull yourself to the 
    enemy and upon contact, flip yourself up and into the air. Press again while ariborn to 
    dive down and slam into the ground. Send out a shockwave dealing damage in an area and
    knocking up enemies.

Special (Threshold):
    Hold to brace yourself for up to two seconds, taking 75% reduced damage. After releasing, 
    or upon reaching the max time, strike forward dealing damage scaling with the damage 
    taken while bracing.
